Statlocker Draft

Classic alternating draft. Teams take turns picking and banning one hero at a time in a strategic interleaved order.

1P → 2P → 1B → 2B → 2P → 1P → 2B → 1B → 1B → 2B → 1P → 2P
SIMULTANEOUS

Phases

Both teams secretly pick 6 heroes (overlaps are banned), then each team bans 4 of the opponent's picks, leaving 2. Then choose your final champion.

Phase 1: Pick 6 → Phase 2: Ban 4 of opponent's → Phase 3: Choose 1
SIMULTANEOUS

Bans First

Ban 3 each first, then pick 5 (overlaps banned), then each team bans 2 from the opponent's picks, then final hero revealed at the end.

Ban 3 → Pick 5 → Opp Bans 2 → Final Pick

✦ DRAFT COMPLETE ✦

Each team plays as their chosen hero — a full squad of 6!
